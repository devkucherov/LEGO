LEGO Group

The LEGO Group was founded in 1932. Its founder was the Danish Ole Kirk Christiansen, who at that time was the foreman of the team of carpenters and joiners. The challenge facing the employees of the company (at first it consisted of only 7 people) was to create toys that develop the imagination, inventiveness and creativity of the child. In 1947, the company expanded its production and began producing plastic toys. Since its inception in 1949, LEGO elements in all their variants have remained compatible with each other. For example, elements created in 1958 still connect with elements released today, despite the radical changes in design and shape of elements over the years.
